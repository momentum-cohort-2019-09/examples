decidor

Decidor is a guided experience that enables users to easily make decisions when faced with a seemingly overwhelming number of choices. The “decidor” app presents users with two options at a time to help them reach an ultimate decision to common daily decisions.

Zero Hour

Zero Hour is a top-down, twin-stick survival laser game. The player must hold off an ever-increasing onslaught of zombies and bosses, protected by only a laser and knife.
